Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pastie AI Agents ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information obtained from users who interact with our services through Facebook Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp, and our live chat widget.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with us through our messaging channels, we may collect:

  • Profile information — your name and profile picture as provided by the platform (Facebook, Instagram)
  • Message content — the text of messages you send to us
  • Platform identifiers — page-scoped user IDs (PSID) assigned by Facebook/Instagram
  • Language preference — automatically detected from your messages
  • Session metadata — timestamps and conversation session IDs

We do not collect payment information, precise location, or device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your identity to our customer support agents in the dashboard
  • To route your conversation to the appropriate support agent
  • To provide AI-assisted responses to your inquiries
  • To translate messages between languages for better communication
  • To analyze conversation topics and improve our service quality

3. Facebook & Instagram Permissions pages_read_engagement

We request the pages_read_engagement permission from Facebook in order to retrieve your public profile name and profile picture when you message our Facebook Page or Instagram account. This information is used solely to identify you in our customer support interface. We do not use this permission to access your posts, friends list, or any other personal data.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase (AWS Southeast Asia)
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized support staff only
  • Conversation data is retained for up to 12 months from the date of last interaction

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers — Google (Gemini AI) for AI-powered responses and translation, under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your conversation history
  • Opt out of AI-automated responses at any time by requesting a human agent

To exercise these rights, contact us at pastiephuquoc@pastie.vn.

7. Cookies

Our live chat widget uses a browser session token (stored in localStorage) to maintain your chat session across page reloads. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
